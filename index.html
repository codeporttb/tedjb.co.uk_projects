Amateur Radio License

Foundation License Preparation

Currently studying for my Amateur Radio Foundation License which will allow me to operate:

  • UHF, VHF, and HF band radios
  • Up to 25W transmission power

The license requires passing a 26-question multiple-choice exam with over 70% score.

There are three levels of UK Amateur Radio licenses:

  1. Foundation
  2. Intermediate
  3. Full

If you're interested in amateur radio, I recommend starting with an SDR (Software Defined Radio) or a LoRa mesh node.

And also make sure you check out essex ham main website for more infomation (link on my home page)
